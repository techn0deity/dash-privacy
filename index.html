DASH — Privacy Policy

Treadmill stats display app
Last updated: 17 May 2026
The short version DASH is designed to be private by default. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. Everything happens on your phone. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Who we are

DASH ("the app") is developed and published as an independent project. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the app handles your data, you can contact us at dash.treadmill@gmail.com.

2. What information DASH handles

DASH is a treadmill stats display app. It connects to compatible Bluetooth treadmills using the standard FTMS (Fitness Machine Service) protocol and shows you real-time information from the treadmill — speed, distance, time, pace, incline, and similar metrics.

2.1 Data stored on your device only

The following information may be stored locally on your phone in the app's storage. None of it leaves your device and we have no way to access it:

  • Your profile — weight, height, age and sex, used solely to calculate calorie burn during your sessions.
  • Paired treadmill information — the Bluetooth address and display name of the treadmill you have chosen to remember, so the app can reconnect to it automatically next time.
  • Session history — for users who have upgraded to DASH Pro, the app saves your completed treadmill sessions (date, duration, distance, calories, average speed, time-series data) so you can review them later.
  • App preferences — your Pro upgrade status and minor display settings.

All of this data is stored in the app's local browser storage on your phone. Uninstalling the app removes all of this data. We do not have a copy.

2.2 Data we do not collect

DASH does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other identifying information
  • Your location, even though Android requires location permission for Bluetooth scanning (see section 4)
  • Any analytics, usage statistics, or device identifiers
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Your treadmill workout data — this stays on your device only

3. Bluetooth connection to your treadmill

DASH reads data directly from your treadmill via a Bluetooth Low Energy connection using the standard FTMS protocol. This communication happens locally between your phone and your treadmill. The data does not travel over the internet and is not sent to us or any third party.

If your device has been granted control of the treadmill, the app can also send control commands (such as setting speed or incline) back to the treadmill at your request.

4. Permissions DASH requests

DASH requests only the minimum permissions needed for its core function. We do not request access to your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, files, or anything beyond what is listed here.

4.1 Bluetooth permissions

Required to scan for and connect to your treadmill. DASH only communicates with treadmills that advertise the FTMS service. Bluetooth data is not shared with anyone.

4.2 Location permission

On Android versions before 12, Google requires apps to hold location permission in order to perform Bluetooth scanning. DASH only needs this for Bluetooth and does not access or use your location at any point. On Android 12 and later, DASH uses the modern Bluetooth permissions, which do not require location access.

5. Pro upgrade and payments

DASH offers a one-time Pro upgrade that unlocks additional features. We plan to use Google Play Billing to process this purchase in a future release.

When Google Play Billing is enabled, all payment information — including your Google account details, payment method, and billing address — is handled directly by Google. DASH never sees, stores, or transmits your payment details. Google's processing of this information is governed by Google's privacy policy.

The only thing DASH stores about your purchase is a local flag on your phone confirming that you have upgraded to Pro. If you uninstall the app, you can restore your purchase from Google Play.

6. Children

DASH is a fitness utility app intended for general use. It is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Data security

Because DASH does not transmit your data anywhere, there is no risk of it being intercepted in transit or breached on our servers — we have no servers.

Data stored locally on your phone is protected by Android's built-in app sandboxing. If your phone is lost, stolen, or compromised, the protection of that data depends on your phone's security settings.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this same URL with an updated "last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to check this page periodically. Significant changes will also be reflected in updates to the app itself.
